Crispy Chicken Wings

Ingredients:

  • 4 chicken legs
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Cooking spray or vegetable oil

Instructions:

Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
Mix the flour, paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, and pepper together in a bowl.
Dip each chicken leg into the buttermilk and then dredge it in the flour mixture until it's coated evenly.
Mix the breadcrumbs, Parmesan cheese, thyme, and oregano together in a separate bowl.
Roll the chicken legs in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing the breadcrumbs onto the chicken to make sure they stick.
Place the chicken legs on a greased or lined baking sheet.
Spray the chicken legs with cooking spray or brush them with vegetable oil to help them brown and crisp up.
Bake the chicken legs in the preheated oven for about 40-45 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the coating is crispy and golden brown.
