Mongolian Rice

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 lb beef or chicken, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions:

Heat the vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over high heat.
Add the beef or chicken and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until browned and cooked through. Remove from the wok and set aside.
Add the garlic, ginger, and onion to the wok and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant and the onion is translucent.
Add the bell pepper and carrot to the wok and stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until they are cooked through but still crisp.
Add the cooked rice to the wok and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until it is heated through and evenly coated with the other ingredients.
Add the soy sauce, hoisin sauce, oyster sauce, sugar, and salt and pepper to taste.
Stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes until the flavors are well combined and the rice is evenly coated.
Add the cooked beef or chicken back to the wok and stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes until it is heated through and evenly distributed.
Serve the Mongolian rice hot, garnished with chopped green onions.
