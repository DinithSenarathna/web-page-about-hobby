Kottu

Ingredients:

  • 4 roti bread (paratha or godhamba roti)
  • 2 cups of boneless chicken, cut into small pieces
  • 1/2 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 green chili, sliced (optional)
  • 1/2 carrot, grated
  • 1/2 cup of cabbage, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of leeks, sliced
  • 1/2 cup of bell pepper, sliced
  • 2 tbsp of oil
  • 1 tbsp of curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp of turmeric powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 eggs, scrambled (optional)
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro

Instructions:

Heat a pan or wok over medium-high heat and add 1 tbsp of oil.
Add chicken to the pan and season with curry powder, turmeric powder, salt, and pepper. Cook until the chicken is no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes.
Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside.
In the same pan, add another tbsp of oil and saut√© onions, garlic, and green chili until fragrant.
Add grated carrot, sliced cabbage, leeks, and bell pepper. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
Add the cooked chicken back into the pan and mix well with the vegetables.
Add the roti bread to the pan and chop into small pieces using a metal spatula or knife.
Mix the roti bread with the chicken and vegetables until everything is well combined.
If desired, add scrambled eggs to the mixture and mix well.
Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve hot.
