Nasi Goreng

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 small onion, diced
  • 2 red chilies, sliced (optional)
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn, green beans)
  • 1 cup diced cooked chicken or shrimp (optional)
  • 2 tablespoons sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon shrimp paste (terasi) (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fried egg (optional)
  • Fried shallots (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (optional)
  • Lime wedges (optional)

Instructions:

Heat the vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
Add the garlic, onion, and red chilies (if using) and stir-fry for 1-2 minutes until fragrant.
Add the mixed vegetables and continue to stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until they are cooked through.
If using cooked chicken or shrimp, add them to the wok and stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes until heated through.
Add the cooked rice to the wok and stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until it is heated through and evenly coated with the other ingredients.
Add the sweet soy sauce, soy sauce, and shrimp paste (if using), and stir-fry for another 1-2 minutes until the flavors are well combined.
Season with salt and pepper to taste.
Serve the Nasi Goreng hot, topped with a fried egg (if desired), fried shallots (if using), fresh cilantro (if using), and lime wedges (if desired) on the side.
