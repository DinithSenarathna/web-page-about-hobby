Vegetable Chopsy

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup finely chopped mixed vegetables (carrots, beans, cabbage, capsicum)
  • 2 medium-sized potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon grated garlic
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon cornflour
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • Oil, for deep frying

Instructions:

Heat a tablespoon of oil in a pan over medium heat.
Add the ginger and garlic and saut√© for a minute.
Add the chopped vegetables to the pan and cook until they are soft and tender.
Add the mashed potatoes, red chili powder, cumin powder, garam masala powder, and salt to the pan. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes until the mixture is dry.
Remove the pan from the heat and allow the mixture to cool.
Take small portions of the mixture and shape them into small balls or cylindrical shapes.
Roll the vegetable balls in cornflour and then in bread crumbs to coat them evenly.
Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat.
Once the oil is hot, add the vegetable chopsey to the pan in small batches and fry until they turn golden brown and crispy.
Remove the chopsey from the oil and drain excess oil on a paper towel.
