Chicken Biriyani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 1 lb chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3-4 green cardamom pods
  • 3-4 cloves
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 2 cups water
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

Rinse the rice in cold water until the water runs clear. Soak the rice in water for 30 minutes.
Heat ghee or oil in a large pot over medium heat. Add the whole spices (cardamom, cloves, cinnamon, bay leaves) and sauté for a few seconds until fragrant.
Add the sliced onions and sauté until golden brown.
Add the chicken pieces and sauté until the chicken is browned on all sides.
Add ginger garlic paste and chopped tomatoes, and sauté for a few more minutes until the tomatoes are soft and the mixture is fragrant.
Add the spices (red chili powder, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala powder, and salt) and mix well.
Add yogurt and mix well. Cook for a few more minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
Drain the rice and add it to the pot with 2 cups of water. Stir well.
Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low and cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid. Cook for 15-20 minutes or until the rice is tender and the water has been absorbed.
Fluff the rice with a fork, and garnish with fresh cilantro before serving.
